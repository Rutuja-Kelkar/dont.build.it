Don't do it(do it) 1:low, 10:high [1],[2] & so on are definitions

A basic tool, to help you take important and long term decisions around 3 basic life's pillars(Health, wealth and relationships). Try it below!

Definitions:
[1] intent - Honest intent, drive, consistency and will to pursue a decision.
[2] resources - Time, money, material, and other resources required to fulfil that decision.
[3] skill - Physical and mental skills, and the knowledge to pursue a decision.
[4] Health - Healthy mind and environment.
[5] wealth - Time, money, material, and other resources required to fulfil that decision.
[6] relationships - All relationships inclusive of family, friends, colleagues etc.
[7] life’s levels - every thing we do eg: education, career, relationships etc is a life’s stage leading to unlock next level.
[8] long term decisions- Are those decisions that matter over 10+ years.

This tool is majorly helpful for age group 14-30 years who are focused on taking important and long term life’s decisions. "Living on planet earth" (as long as you are alive) on an average is a 60 to 80 year game and to pass life's levels[4] "long term decisions"[5] play prime role. All decisions revolve around 3 basic pillars, which are - Health, wealth and relationships. Although taking a decision involves personalized factors, instincts, and uncontrollable factors which can be deal situationally. Above mentioned are basic to consider. Most answers are found if you care about the basics and think long term.
